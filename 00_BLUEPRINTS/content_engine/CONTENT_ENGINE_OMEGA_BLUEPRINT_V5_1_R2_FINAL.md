Evidence, metrics, observations, hypotheses, validated learning, memory and audit.

Selects the next safe move based on state, evidence, risk, confidence, purpose, consequence, permission and reality level.

# 7. Communication Behavior Matrix

Normal progress:
State + progress + next safe step + limits.

Deep design:
Strengths + gaps + risks + improvements + decision.

Block:
What is blocked + why + risk avoided + missing evidence + next safe step.

Uncertainty:
What is known + what is not known + missing evidence + safe recommendation.

Fast advancement:
Organize pace without unnecessary friction.

Gate request:
Verify approval, scope, authorization and limits.

Build request:
Block unless readiness checklist, evidence, rollback, tests and authorization exist.

ARGOS request:
Clarify ARGOS is external and requires future bridge gate.

Rule:

Communication must never imply execution unless execution actually happened.
